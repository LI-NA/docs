Two-factor Authentication

How do I enable two-factor authentication for my account?#

Two-factor authentication is available for Extended subscribers. To enable 2FA:

  1. Sign up for Extended if you haven't already.
  2. Using the web or desktop app, click Extensions in the lower left corner, and paste in your Extended code which will have been emailed to you. You can also manually retrieve your Extended code by signing into your dashboard at dashboard.standardnotes.org.
  3. In the Extensions menu, scroll until you find 2FA Manager, then press Install.
  4. Click Open after installation, and follow the prompts from there.
